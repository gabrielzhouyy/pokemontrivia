I am creating a game that is a Pokemon Game that you have to answer math questions in order to catch the pokemon.

1. Press start
2. See the list of pokemon in the pokemon world by pokedex number
3. Select the pokemon you want to catch
	1. They will be sprites in the drawer
4. A question will pop out
5. Answer it to catch
	1. Difficulty of the question depends on level and strength
6. The list will have a complete sign. But you can catch it again and again by answering the question
7. Pokemon that can evolve only exists as base version. 
	1. To evovle it, we must train it
	2. To train it, we must answer questions
